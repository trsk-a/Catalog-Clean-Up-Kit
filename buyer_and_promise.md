# Buyer and Promise

## Primary Buyer
- Visual freelancer serving small online stores.
- Small to medium e-commerce seller (Etsy, Shopify, Amazon, marketplace).
- Small business with simple product catalog and no visual R&D team.

## Buyer Job-To-Be-Done
"I need usable product images quickly without building ComfyUI from scratch or troubleshooting node/version chaos."

## Exact Promise
"Improve product photos for catalog, online store, and social with a pre-configured ComfyUI system that is installable, documented, and tuned by hardware tier."

## Non-Promise Clauses
- Not a replacement for premium studio photography campaigns.
- Not guaranteed for out-of-scope materials and scenes.
- Not guaranteed for chaotic legacy ComfyUI environments.

## Success Criteria (Buyer Perspective)
- First usable output in under 20 minutes after clean install.
- Clear preset choice by VRAM tier.
- Reliable pass/fail guidance from output checklist.
- Fewer manual fixes on average catalog deliverables.
