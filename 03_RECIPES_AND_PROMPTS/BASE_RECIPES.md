# Base Recipes

## Recipe 01 - Clean Catalog Neutral
- Intent: clean product, neutral look, conservative enhancement.
- Positive seed prompt: "clean product photo, realistic shape, neutral studio light, commercial catalog style, controlled shadow, no distortion"
- Suggested denoise: 0.30-0.36
- Use case: default for rigid objects.

## Recipe 02 - Soft E-commerce Boost
- Intent: slightly richer contrast and presence while staying natural.
- Positive seed prompt: "e-commerce product photo, natural surface cleanup, crisp but realistic edges, simple shadow"
- Suggested denoise: 0.33-0.40
- Use case: small catalog refresh where source is usable but flat.

## Recipe 03 - Recovery Moderate Input
- Intent: salvage moderate imperfections without over-stylization.
- Positive seed prompt: "product photo restoration for catalog, preserve original identity, remove small defects, consistent texture"
- Suggested denoise: 0.28-0.35
- Use case: noisy/lightly underlit but in-scope photos.
