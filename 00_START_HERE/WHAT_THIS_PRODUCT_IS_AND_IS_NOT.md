# What This Product Is and Is Not

## Is
- A narrow, reproducible ComfyUI system for product photo clean-up.
- Built for rigid and semi-rigid objects in controlled scenarios.
- Focused on catalog, e-commerce, marketplace, and static social deliverables.

## Is Not
- A universal product photography engine.
- A lifestyle scene generator.
- A guaranteed solution for jewelry complexity, difficult transparency, or mirror-grade reflective products.
