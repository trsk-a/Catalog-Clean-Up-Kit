# V1 In Scope / Out of Scope

## In Scope
- Main clean-up workflow for rigid/semi-rigid product photos.
- Neutral background workflow.
- Export/validation utility workflow.
- VRAM presets (8/12/16+ GB).
- Base recipes and negative prompts.
- Compatibility matrix and dependency lock references.
- Clean install guides (Windows/Linux).
- Existing-install disclaimers and recovery guidance.
- Troubleshooting master.
- QA matrix and 10-case template.

## Out of Scope
- Universal product-photo engine.
- Jewelry complex surfaces and gemstone critical fidelity.
- Fashion on-body processing.
- Difficult transparency and liquid-heavy cases.
- Reflective mirror-grade products as guaranteed category.
- Packaging with critical tiny text accuracy requirements.
- Complex lifestyle scene generation.

## Decision Rule
If a case needs universal realism or physically accurate high-complexity behavior, reject it for V1.
