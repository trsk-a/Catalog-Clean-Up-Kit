# Landing Copy Draft

## Headline
Clean Product Photos Faster With a ComfyUI Kit That Actually Ships With Rules, Presets, and Troubleshooting.

## Subheadline
Catalog Clean-Up Kit V1 helps freelancers and small e-commerce teams improve rigid and semi-rigid product photos for catalog, marketplace, and static social outputs without building a pipeline from zero.

## Promise
Install, choose your VRAM preset, run the workflow, validate output with checklist, export in standard ratios.

## What You Get
- Main clean-up workflow.
- Neutral background workflow.
- Export/validation utility.
- Presets for 8/12/16+ GB.
- Recipes + negative prompts.
- Compatibility matrix and install guides.
- Troubleshooting docs and QA framework.

## Scope Transparency
Not a universal product-photography tool. V1 excludes jewelry complexity, fashion-on-body, difficult transparency, mirror-grade reflective guarantees, and complex lifestyle scenes.
