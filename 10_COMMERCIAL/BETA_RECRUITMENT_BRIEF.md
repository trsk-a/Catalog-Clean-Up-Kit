# Beta Recruitment Brief

## Target Tester Profile
- Freelancer or small e-commerce operator.
- Uses product photos for catalog/shop/social static.
- Has 8 GB, 12 GB, or 16+ GB VRAM setup.

## Ask
Run 2-3 in-scope cases and return:
- input category
- hardware tier
- output classification
- top issue (if any)
- time to first usable result

## Exclude Testers Looking For
- jewelry-critical fidelity
- fashion-on-body pipelines
- complex reflective/transparency promises
