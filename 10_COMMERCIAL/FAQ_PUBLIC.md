# Public FAQ

## What is this for?
Improving product photos for catalog/e-commerce/social static.

## What is it not for?
Universal all-product processing, complex lifestyle scenes, and high-complexity reflective/transparency categories.

## What GPU do I need?
8 GB minimum tier; 12 GB recommended; 16+ GB for quality reference.

## I already have ComfyUI. Can I install this?
Yes, with limited-support existing-install path; clean install is recommended.

## Why was my case rejected?
Likely out-of-scope category or input quality outside acceptance envelope.
